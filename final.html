Welcome to my Final Project Page!

For my final project I created an educational program meant for younger kids who need help learning their shapes.
This program is quite easy to use. Once it is opened just select a shape to be drawn and then pick a color to trace
and hold the left mouse to draw with the color that was selected. The shape can be traced as many times and with
however many colors that is desired. When you want to have a fresh start on the shape click the clear button. When you
want to pick a new shape click the restart button and you will be back at the beginning to start the same process over
again. Instructions are also on each screen to help. Have fun!
